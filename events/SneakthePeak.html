Sneak the Peak

    June 20, 2021 Venue - IIC Hall

let’s touch the sky by blending the extract of chemicals.

What is Sneak The Peak?

The reaction of chemistry is so interesting and become more excited when you perform in the laboratory. Some of its reactions leave question marks that how this type of occurs. Some of these types of reactions are dancing gummy bears, fire rainbow, sugar snakes and etc. Elephant toothpaste is part of the reaction. We organize an event for Elephant toothpaste.



Flow Of Events

  • This event is for the testing of practical knowledge of chemical reagents and reaction and participants have to make elephant toothpaste of maximum with using prefect proportion of chemical reagents.
  • It is a single-participant event.
  • We organize a quiz round for testing participants’ knowledge of chemistry and chemical reactions.
  • Firstly participants’ have to find the potassium reagents among 5 or 6 other reagent by their color and smell specification. And then they perform elephant toothpaste reaction.
  • The prize will be distributed to the top 3 participants and all others who will be complete the task will also be awarded.

    • Rules and Regulations

    • Every Participant can take only 200ml of H2O2 as a maximum amount.
    • Every Participant can take only 10ml of Potassium reagents as a maximum amount.
    • Every participant have to perform the task within 15 min otherwise He/She will be disqualified.
    • Undergraduate students from Indian colleges, with knowledge of basic chemical reactions.

      • Judging Criteria

      • In the first round top 15 participants, who gave the maximum number of right answers will qualify for the second round.
      • Whose elephants toothpaste attains maximum height will announce as a winner.
      • In case two or more participants' elephant toothpaste attains the same maximum height then the winning criteria will depend on the time span within the task will perform.
