What is Circuit Cracker?

Do you want to know where we use circuits, the elixir of an electronics engineer in our daily life? Then this event is best suited for you! Test your design, simulation and hardware implementation skills with the real life problems that will be given to you. Circuit has to be designed according to the given problem statement to test the participants circuit designing ability. The designed circuit must meet the needs of the application given.


Flow of Event

  • The participants will be given complete details about the event and its rules, score counting procedure, negatives, and elimination.They will also be given details of the breadboard and basic idea about the circuit construction on the breadboard.
  • The participants will be making a team constituting 2 to 5 members and register for the event.Registration will be done for maximum 20 teams on basis of first come first.
  • In this round Teams will be given two problem statement one after another. It will be of maximum 30 minutes and the First 10 Teams with well defined and systematic explanation of the circuit on A4 sheet will qualify for the next round andy team failed to explain the circuit inspite of desining it will be eliminated. Any kind of malpractice, use of mobile phones, calculators,cheating, and copying will led to direct elimination of the team.
  • In this round Teams will be given an advanced brain storming problem statement for maximum duration of 30minutes. Teams will be designing the circuit and the first 3 teams along with circuit diagram and complete explanation of the circuit and the circuit elements will be declared winners.
  • Prize will be distributed to First 3 teams qualifying the Final round.

Rules and Regulations of the event

  • All team members participating in the event should have coordination among themselves.
  • No one is allowed to help other team members except his/her own team.
  • Participants are required to maintain discipline and decorum while working.
  • Use of any kind of electronic gadget is prohibited.
  • Participants should have their institute I'd card with them, throughout the event.
  • One team can have a minimum of members and maximum of 5 members.
