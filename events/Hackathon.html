Hackathon

    June 20, 2021 Venue - IIC Hall

An idea that is not dangerous is unworthy of being called an idea at all.

What is Hackathon?

Hackathon is an event conducted with the idea to bring people from different walks of life, possessing different sets of skills, to work together and solve some real-world problems using their attained skills. In every aspect of life we saw the many problems which bother us every day and we are unable to do about them. But our mind making a solution for that and this event is for implementation of these type of ideas for real life problems.


Flow of Event

  • Teams need to register through the registration form which will be released. The teams will need to fill their team leader and other member details. They will also need to submit their model idea in brief.
  • The team will be given ample time of 2 days to build the software and hardware.
  • Event will be only of a round which will run for 3 hours.
  • All the teams are required to gather at the specified labs to work on the problem and mentor walkthrough.
  • The participants will be free to work at the specified labs in the time given.

Rules and Regulations of the event

  • Registering for the hackathon is entirely free of charge.
  • Each team must be given a name as chosen by its members. The team’s name must be neutral in nature and must not be too flashy. Team size should be of 3 - 5 members.
  • Participating teams are required to provide correct information about each and every team member during the online application. Any ambiguous entry will not be considered.
  • Eligible Submissions will be evaluated by a panel of judges selected by the Administrator (the “Judges”). Judging may take place in one or more rounds with one or more panels of Judges, at the discretion of the Administrator.
  • Judges are not required to test the Application and may choose to judge based solely on the text description, images, and video provided in the Submission.
  • Administrators reserve the right in their sole discretion to disqualify any individual or Maker it finds to be actually or presenting the appearance of tampering with the entry process or the operation of the Hackathon or to be acting in violation of these Official Rules or in a manner that is inappropriate, not in the best interests of this Hackathon, or a violation of any applicable law or regulation.
  • You will be bound by and comply with these Official Rules and the decisions of the Administrator, and/or the Hackathon Judges which are binding and final in all matters relating to the Hackathon.
  • You will be bound by and comply with these Official Rules and the decisions of the Administrator, and/or the Hackathon Judges which are binding and final in all matters relating to the Hackathon.
  • Participating teams are required to coordinate with the management team of IIC for the smooth execution of the event.
